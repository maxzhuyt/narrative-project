In  it,  Mrs.  Gaines  read  an  article  about  pernicious  anemia.  Her  secret  and  baseless  convic-  tion  had  long  been  that  her  body,  round  and  firm  as  it  was,  could  never  last  out  the  natural  term  of  a  life  as  wonderful  as  hers.
