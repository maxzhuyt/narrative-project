When  he  spoke,  the  words  came  huskily.  &quot;Throw  away!&quot;  he  croaked.  &quot;Poi-  son!&quot;  The  hostess  disappeared,  carrying  the  fateful  bottle.  When  the  plane  reached  New  York,  my  companion  was  still  in  pain.  He  was  barely  able  to  thank  me  for  my  services  as  guide.  'The  morning  air,  taken  neat,  had  been  overpowering;  when  one's  system  is  long  deprived  of  that  elixir,  which  alone  has  the  power  to  cure  the  general  sickness,  the  shock  of  the  first  drink  is  great.  'The  truth  is,  the  journey  had  been  almost  too  much  for  both  of  us.  I  think  it  may  well  be  my  last  trip  to  the  pond.  Perhaps  it  was  just  because  of  the  presence  of  the  Senator,  but  the  frogs  sounded  all  the  same  as  I  listened-no  variation  in  their  voices.  It  didn't  used  to  bethat  way,  and  I  don't  like  such  ponds."
