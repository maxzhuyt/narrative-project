That  was  what  happened  to  Pritam.  As  the  years  went  by  and  she  continued  living  by  herself,  her  ap-  pearance  became  rougher  and  shabbier,  and  though  she  was  still  in  her  thirties,  she  completely  forgot  to  care  for  her-  self  or  think  about  herself  as  a  physical  person.
