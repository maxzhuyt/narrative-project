From  a  musical  family  of  physicists,  he  had  played  the  bass  recorder  since  childhood,  having  been  trained  in  this  instrument  to  round  out  a  quartet  consisting  of  his  father  (tenor),  mother  (soprano),  and  sister  (alto).
