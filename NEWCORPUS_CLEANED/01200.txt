Instead  of  travelling  on  the  midnight  flight,  the  three  of  them-Dr.  Florence,  Ms.  Bianca,  and  the  Professor-are  stay-  ing  on  at  the  hotel  after  the  champagne  and  the  final  session,  the  dinner  dance.
