The  time  was  late  October,  and  the  weather  was  crisp  and  obliging.  By  the  third  day,  the  strikers  had  been  through  one  round  of  picket  duty. Moss,  the  captain  of  Picket  Team  D,  arrived  in  the  rain  on  the  strike's  fourth  day  for  the  4-8  a.m.  shift.
