We  trailed  slowly  up  the  stairs  while  Aunt  Nelly  dashed  about  down-  stairs  closing  doors  and  emitting  cries  of  good  night.  Our  shadows  leaped  ahead  of  us.  When  my  mother  and  I  were  inside  the  white  bedroom  we  shared,  with  only  the  sound  that  the  sea  made  slapping  against  the  rocks,  the  shadows  would  mount  the  walls  mysteriously  as  we  undressed.
