My  poor  brother  Sampson,  who  appears  as  &quot;Rafe,&quot;  is  found,  in  the  first  chapter,  performing  a  laparoscopy  upon  a  patient  who  had  been  under  the  impression  she  was  paying  for  quite  another  procedure. What  the  book  doesnot  say  is  that  the  lapa-  roscopy  disclosed  a  fair  amount  of  en-  dometriosis  which  was  then  dealt  with  in  a  highly  skilled  and  professional  manner,  thus  averting  considerable  pa-  tient  disgruntlement.
