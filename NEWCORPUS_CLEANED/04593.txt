In  appearance,  she  was  thin  and  of  medium  height,  with  blue  eyes  and  wavy  hairthat  had  turned  prematurely  white.  She  had  an  aquiline  nose,  an  erect  carriage,  and  she  wore  simple  but  fashionable  clothes.  She  married  at  twenty-one  a  man  of  the  same  age,  bore  three  children,  and  after  her  husband's  death,  atthirty-five,  never  remarried. My  grandfather  had  been  a  crack  shot  and  had  trained  his  daughter  as  well  as  his  son  from  childhood  in  the  use  of  firearms.
