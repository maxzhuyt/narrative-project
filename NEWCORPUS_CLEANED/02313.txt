He  looked  amazed.  She  got  out  of  the  car  and  shut  the  door.  He  took  off  like  a  bank  robber.  Carol  watched  the  car  speed  away.  He  was  going  home  to  his  waiting  mother.  Carol  thought  that  there  was  something  touching  about  him.  He  was  kind  and  sensitive.  Then  she  con-  sidered  his  age.  It  was  impossible.  She  might  go  out  with  him  again,  but  she  knew  that  noth-  ing  could  come  of  it.  She  hurried  up  the  stone  path.  Her  breath  plumed.  She  looked  up  at  the  stars.  She  thought  of  the  words  &quot;killer  waitress&quot;  and  shivered.  She  knew  she  was  going  to  have  trouble  getting  to  sleep.
