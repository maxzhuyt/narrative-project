I  was  in  my  last  year  of  residency  at  the  Hospital  of  the  City  of  [REDACTED]  when  I  learned  to  my  sorrow  that  old  Dr.  Frank,  the  town's  Jewish  doctor  for  more  than  fifty  years,  had  died.
