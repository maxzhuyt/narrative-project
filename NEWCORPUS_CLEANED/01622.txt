I  knew  him  only  as  he  appeared  in  a  single  photo-  graph.  This  picture,  technically  black-  and-white,taken  of  a  blond  officer  in  uniform,  seemed  to  have  acquired  the  khaki  tones  of  its  military  subject. So  radi-  ant  was  he,  he  might  have  been  the  light  that  blanched  the  negative. We  broke  out  of  a  holding  pattern  of  livingin  relatives'  apart-  ments  throughout  the  city,  and  moved  to  a  studio  of  our  own.
