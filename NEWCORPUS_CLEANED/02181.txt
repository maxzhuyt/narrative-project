Hooper  lights  a  cigar  and  crosses  his  legs.  That  morning  he  sold  a  com-  puter  company  in  Austin,  [REDACTED],  eighty-seven  thousand  dollars'  worth  of  letterhead  stationery  and  bills  of  lading.  He  will  win  another  Hooper  if  the  other  salesmen  are  not  careful.
