In  spite  of  his  admonition,  I  have  been  like  him.  In  many  ways.  We  had  a  good  time  dreaming,  but  that  is  not  at  all  the  picture  I  have  had  of  him  or  me.  Now  there  is  a  little  stir  against  the  moonlight.  Miles  is  coming  toward  me.  His  step  is  light.  His  mustache  is  trim.  His  tie  is  a  glory.  &quot;Why,  hello,  Cartie,&quot;  he  says.  &quot;I  haven't  seen  you  for  a  while.&quot;  "
