When  we  were  becalmed  trying  to  get  around  it,  the  sound  of  the  bell  nearly  drove  us  wild.  Amazing  what  a  different  sound  it  has  over  water.  Flat,for  one  thing-  no  ring  to  it,  no  overtones.  Harsh,  just  a  clap  and  then  gone  and  the  next  one  to  come.  And  four  clappers  going  full  time.  Here,  heard  through  the  trees  outside  my  windows,  the  bell  is  a  chord  on  B  flat.  The  ring  lingers.  The  sound  is  distant  and  it  is  soft.  Each  time,  it  hangs  in  the  air  so  long  it  seems  it  always  will.  Before  it  ends,  the  next  ring  comes-so  it  does.
