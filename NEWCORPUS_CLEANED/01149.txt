He  would  smile  and  nod  and,  seeing  the  nod  reproduced  in  canted  glass,  would  verbalize  it  into  &quot;Very  nice&quot;  or  &quot;Much  neater&quot;  or  &quot;Just  the  job&quot;  or  &quot;Thank  you.&quot;  If  they  had  clipped  a  swastika  into  his  nape,  he  would  probably  have  pretended  to  approve.  Then,  one  day,  he  thought,  No,  I  don't  want  to  see  the  back.  If  the  front's  O.K.,  the  back  will  be,  too.  That  wasn't  pre-  tentious,  was  it?  No,  it  was  logical.  He  was  rather  proud  of  his  initiative.  Of  course,  Kelly  always  forgot,  but  that  didn't  matter.  In  fact,  it  was  better,  in  a  way,  since  it  meant  that  his  timid  victory  was  repeated  every  time.
