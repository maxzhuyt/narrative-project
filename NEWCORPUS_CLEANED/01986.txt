Freddie  had  been  in  the  hospital  for  five  days.  The  first  morning  she  had  woken  to  the  sound  of  squawking.  Since  her  room  was  next  door  to  the  nursery,  she  as-  sumed  this  was  a  sound  some  new-  borns  made.  The  next  day  she  got  out  of  bed  at  dawn  and  saw  that  the  meadow  was  full  of  seagulls  who  con-  gregated  each  morning  before  the  sun  came  up.
