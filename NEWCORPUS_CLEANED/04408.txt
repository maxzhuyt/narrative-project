I  was  busy  there,  and  more  than  a  year  passed  after  the  unveiling  of  the  Tiergarten  Memorial  before  I  found  time  to  go  and  see  it.  By  then,a  strange  thing  had  started  to  happen.  The  out-  stretched  arm  of  the  huge  statue  had  be-  gun  to  collapse  slowly  under  its  own  weight-a  development  that  was  giving  rise  to  much  merriment  and  topical  comment  in  Western  Sector  news-  papers.  I  found  the  monument  surrounded  by  scaffolding,  and  I  also  noticed  that  Isaac's  prediction  about  the  marble  had  been  all  too  accurate;  whole  slabs  of  it  on  the  pedestal  were  discolored  and  were  crumbling  away.
