Ten-year-old  boys,  when  they  set  out  upon  men's  business,  have  a  sense  of  the  man  that  is  growing  in  them.  Our  silence  was  in  honor  of  those  old,  solitary  longshoremen  we  passed,  many  of  them  our  relatives,  whose  boats  were  beached  on  shingle  where,one  day,  our  boats  would  be.
