Irrationally,  she  believed  that  her  depar-  ture  itself  represented  such  an  undertak-  ing,  and  that  it  would  have  been  possible  for  her  to  stay,  protected,  in  the  flat  green  garden  between  the  two  lines  of  moun-  tains  without  ever  fully  acknowledging  what  had  brought  her  there.
