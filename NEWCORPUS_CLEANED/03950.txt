The  bed  is  comfortable.  Paul  settles  into  it,  turning  on  his  side  and  locking  a  pillow  in  the  crook  of  his  arm.  I'm  get-  ting  older,  he  thinks.  I  can't  leave  things  in  the  air  much  longer.  I've  got  to  make  up  my  mind.  ...  But  soon  sleep  bends  over  him,  and  Paul,  drawing  the  pillow  to  his  breast,  submits  to  her.  Something  is  bound  to  happen,  he  thinks.  Some-  thing  will  happen  soon,  and  then  I  will  decide."
