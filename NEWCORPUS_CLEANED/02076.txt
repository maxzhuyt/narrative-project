Ada,  after  going  to  the  window  and  making  certain  that  it  was  the  evening  sun  and  not  a  brush  fire  burning  among  the  gray  trees  beyond  the  old  fir,  turned  on  her  radio  and  sat  down  to  hear  the  last  of  the  Christmas  mu-  sic.
