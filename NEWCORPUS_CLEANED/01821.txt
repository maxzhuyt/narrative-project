It  was  more  central.  And  meeting  Mrs.  Mi-  lan  there  was  no  accident;  I  knew  her  work  schedule.  She  passed  my  machine  shop  and  even  stopped  in  if  her  hus-  band  happened  to  be  there,for  a  job  on  his  tractor  or  whatever.  I  knew  that  on  her  way  home  on  Friday,  whenever  the  weather  was  good,  she  stopped  at  the  Eight  Mile  and  took  off  her  shoes  and  climbed  down  the  gully  to  stand  with  her  feet  in  the  water.  She  never  stayed  long-hardly  long  enough  to  think  about  being  there-but  she  could  be  relied  on  to  take  that  break,  and  it  was  easy  enough  one  Friday  afternoon  to  take  it  with  her.
