She  had  dropped  the  subject  and  the  point  had  never  been  settled.  Gloss  over  all  trou-  ble,  and  back  to  the  endless  pretense  of  hand  in  hand.  Gloss  over  marriage.  And,  hazily,  on  the  edge  of  sleep  but  unable  to  sleep,  unsteady  with  exhaus-  tion,  Mark  thought  that  it  was  not  necessarily  quarrels  they  had  lacked  but  simply  obstacles.  Opposition,  some-  thing  to  grow  against,  like  soil  above  a  seed.  He  longed  to  say  so  to  her.  Now  the  flight  was  well  along. He  put  the  wallet  away  and  leaned  back,  his  eyes  open  but  unfocussed.
