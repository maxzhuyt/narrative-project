Descartes  hinted  at  the  problem  when  he  wrote,  &quot;My  mind  can  never  know  my  body,  although  it  has  become  quite  friendly  with  my  legs.&quot;  By  &quot;knowable,&quot;  incidentally,  I  do  not  mean  that  which  can  be  known  by  perception  of  the  senses,  or  that  which  can  be  grasped  by  the  mind,  but  more  that  which  can  be  said  to  be  Known  or  to  possess  a  Knownness  or  Knowability,  or  at  least  something  you  can  mention  to  a  friend.
