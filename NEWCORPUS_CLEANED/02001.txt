The  results  of  the  knitting  proofreader's  work  were  sold  by  the  company  at  a  quar-  terly  sale  from  which  she  gained  nei-  ther  garb  nor  penny.  But  she  remained  the  most  blithe  person  in  the  place:  as  good  as  the  comps  at  the  printer's.
