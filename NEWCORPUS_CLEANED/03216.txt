Each  year,  he  argued  with  her-that  the  next  apartment  and  landlord  would  be  no  better  than  the  last,  that  he  was  tired  of  moving,  particularly  since  she  moved  so  many  shopping  bags  and  cardboard  boxes  and  admittedly  did  not  know  what  was  in  half  the  boxes  but  simply  moved  them  from  the  closet  of  one  apartment  to  the  closet  of  another.  Each  year  he  argued,  until  finally,  after  thirteen  moves,  he  became  ada-  mant,  and  put  his  foot  down.  He  would  not  move  again.
