Lying  in  bed  beside  him,  I  could  still  picture  it-I  could  even  feel  it:  Keith  and  I  standing  together,the  lights  below  us,  the  wind  in  our  hair,  and  in  all  the  clouds  around  us  the  snow  just  beginning  to  form,  collecting  itself,  waiting  to  fall.
