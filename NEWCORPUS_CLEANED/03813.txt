He  had  found  the  house  while  hunt-  ing,  deep  in  the  tract  of  second-  or  third-growth  forest  owned  by  a  steel  company.  The  steel  company  was  at  the  other  end  of  the  state,  in  Pitts-  burgh.  Fifteen  years  ago  it  had  bought  local  land  wholesale,  on  the  speculation  that  it  contained  low-grade  iron  ore.  The  company  had  not  yet  mined  it  and  perhaps  never  would. How  old  was  it?  The  trees  around  it  were  tall  but  not  thick,  and  a  vestigial  farm-  yard  remained,  earth  too  packed  to  en-  courage  roots.
