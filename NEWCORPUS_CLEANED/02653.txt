It  is  impossible  to  ask,  and  so  Yvonne  frowns,  unseen,  in  the  gathering  dusk-both  at  the  ambiguity  and  at  the  surprise  of  it.  And  she,too,  says  something  new:  &quot;Ah,  Matthew,  what  an  absolute  fool  you  are.&quot;  But  she  has  said  it  lightly,  and  sheadds,  &quot;You  would  have  got  along  perfectly  well  without  me.&quot;  She  knows  that  out  of  her  true  fondness  for  Matthew  she  has  lied,  and  that  it  is  still  necessary  for  her  to  survive  him.
