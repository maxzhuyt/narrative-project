Not  such  a  bad  birthday  after  all,  he  thought,  and  unwrapped  the  paint-  er's  present.  It  was  the  wet-fields  painting,  Oliver  was  not  too  surprised  to  discover.  And  just  what  was  he  supposed  to  do  with  it?  he  wondered.  It  wasn't  particularly  well  painted.  In  fact,  the  painter  himself  had  seemed  dissatisfied  with  it.  Oliver  felt  a  slight  surge  of  irritation  at  being  given  a  picture  that  the  painter  had  been  un-  ableto  finish  properly.  It  needed  some-  thing  else  in  it,  not  just  fields  and  sky.  Maybe,  Oliver  thought,  he  should  paint  his  bike  in  one  of  the  corners,  have  it  leaning  over  on  its  stand  ...  The  girl  in  the  bikini  turned  over  suddenly  and  rolled  onto  her  small  dog,  who  gave  an  anguished  yelp  of  pain  and  surprise.
