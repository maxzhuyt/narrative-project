She  hit  the  Groaner,  and  went  down  with  all  hands.&quot;  &quot;Sunk  without  trace?&quot;  Jim  asked,  after  a  short  silence.  &quot;Never  found  a  stick  of  her,  or  any  sign  of  the  crew,&quot;  Ray  said,  his  eyes  on  the  buoy.  &quot;Did  Sylvia  ever  hear  about  it?&quot;  Jim  asked  cautiously.  He  saw,  with  re-  lief,  that  Ray  was  smiling.  ''Yes,&quot;  Ray  said.  &quot;Sylvia  knows  the  whole  story.&quot;  &quot;I'm  glad  of  that,&quot;  Jim  said.  &quot;I'd  hate  to  have  been  the  one  to  have  to  tell  her.&quot;"
