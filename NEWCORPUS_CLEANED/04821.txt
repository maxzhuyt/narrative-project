At  first  glance,  it  looks  conventional  enough.  It  measures  nine  by  twelve  inches,  has  a  yellow  cover,  and  is  called  &quot;Our  Baby's  First  Seven  Years.&quot;  So  far,  so  good.  Many  of  its  pages  seem  harmless  enough,  too,  on  a  quick  leaf-through.  But  the  baby-book  makers  have  become  dedicated.
