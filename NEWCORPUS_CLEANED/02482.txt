Dinah  studied  the  picture  for  a  long  while,  so  that  by  the  time  her  fever  returned  and  she  began  to  drift  into  a  light  sleep,  she  had  become  con-  vinced  that  she  did,  indeed,  know  this  girl.  The  girl  was  Sophie,  beloved  of  Dale,  loping  steadily  toward  him,  or  perhaps  away  from  him,  through  that  neighborhood,  to  be  loved  or  not  to  be  loved  to  death. "
