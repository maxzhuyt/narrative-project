It  was  I  who  was  someone  else  to  her  now,  and  myself  to  myself.  So  the  story  of  these  last  months  came  out  piecemeal-the  salient  facts,  the  dramatis  personae.  But-how  do  you  tell  a  whole  person  what  itis  like  to  break  apart,  to  be  more  than  one?  That  centrifugal  forces  can  get  so  out  of  hand?  That  selves  are  facets  of  self?  How  do  you  justify  flight  to  someone  who  does  not  flee?  Does  not?  But  then-where  is  Erika  now?  So.  She  is  one  of  us  after  all.  Flight.  And  return.  She  will  be  back.
