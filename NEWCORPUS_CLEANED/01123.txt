In  my  waking  hours  I  am  Professor  Emeritus  Hermann  Sorgel;  I  putter  about  the  card  catalogue  and  compose  erudite  trivialities,  but  at  dawn  I  sometimes  know  that  the  person  dreaming  is  that  other  man.  Every  so  often  in  the  evening  I  am  unsettled  by  small,  fleeting  memories  that  are  perhaps  authentic.
