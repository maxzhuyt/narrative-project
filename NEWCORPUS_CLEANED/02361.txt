Henry  said  it  was  a  good  life  if  you  didn't  weaken.  Morgan  said  he  wished  us  all  the  most  sincere  Merry  Christmas.  When  we  came  out  of  the  [REDACTED]  Barn  it  was  snowing.  Lily  said  it  was  like  a  Christmas  card,  and  so  it  was,  with  the  snow  whirling  around  the  street  lights  in  town  and  around  the  colored  lights  people  had  put  up  out-  side  their  doorways.  Morgan  was  giv-  ing  Henry  and  Irene  a  ride  home  in  the  truck,  acknowledging  age  and  pregnancy  and  Christmas.  Morgy  took  a  shortcut  through  the  field,  and  Herb  walked  off  by  himself,  head  down  and  hands  in  his  pockets,  roll-  ing  slightly,  as  if  he  were  on  the  deck  of  a  lake  boat.
