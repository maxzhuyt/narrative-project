Rachel  laughed  and  laughed  until  her  eyes  filled  with  tears.  &quot;That,&quot;  she  said  in  between  great  gulps  of  air,  &quot;is...  the...  most...  ro-  mantic...  thing...  anyone's  ever  said  to  me.&quot;  Will  sat  there  helplessly,  feeling  like  the  stupidest  man  in  the  world,  but  when  things  calmed  down  again  they  seemed  to  be  in  a  different  place.  &quot;You  don't  need  a  point,&quot;  Rachel  told  him.  &quot;Don't  I?  That's  not  what  it  feels  like.&quot;  &quot;No,  no.  The  point  is  you  keep  go-  ing.
