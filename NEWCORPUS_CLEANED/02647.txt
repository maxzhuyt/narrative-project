And  he  toldusthat  once,  during  the  May  Bumps,  he  had  borrowed  a  pair  of  trousers  from  Forster,  since  the  two  men  were  the  same  size,  and  they  fitted  him  perfectly,  breaking  just  over  the  shoes-which,  I  hope,  forever  disposes  of  that  balderdash  about  their  being  three  inches  too  short.  So  then,  to  round  things  off,  we  signed  Victoria  Glendinning's  name  to  the  luncheon  check,  in  careof  The  Times  Literary  Supplement.  Perhaps  that  will  teach  her  to  keep  mum  on  matters  she  knows  nothing  about.  "
