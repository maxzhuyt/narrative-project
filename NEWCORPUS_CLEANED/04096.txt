He  knew  I  didn't  mean  it,  but  he  was  shocked,  though  a  few  months  before  the  suggestion  would  have  seemed  reasonable  to  him.  I  told  myself  it  was  just  the  other  side  of  the  medal.  Where  love  is,  there  is  hate  also.
