His  eyes  came  to  rest  on  her  where  she  lay  across  the  courtyard  in  a  stripe  of  sun-  light,  curled  in  the  dirt  with  her  knees  drawn  up  beneath  her  and  her  chin  rest-  ing  atop  the  cradle  of  her  crossed  wrists.  He  watched  her  for  a  long  while  as  she  lay  motionless  there,  no  more  aware  of  what  she  was  than  a  dog  or  an  ass,  and  he  felt  defeated,  defeated  and  depressed.
