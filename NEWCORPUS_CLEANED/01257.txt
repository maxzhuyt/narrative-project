Daddy!&quot;  I  started  running  toward  her  voice  and,  forgetting  everything  that  had  happened,  I  burst  through  the  blanks  of  the  men  to  the  only  thing  that  mattered  and  swept  my  daughter  off  the  horse  and  into  my  arms.
