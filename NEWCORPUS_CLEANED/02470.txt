I  can't  make  him  see  that  with  only  the  two  of  us  left  in  the  house  we  don't  need  any  more  than  these  in  the  cupboards  and  the  dozen  pots  of  pur\xc3\xa9e  we've  got  in  the  deep-  freeze.
