An Unforgetable Day

Yesterday, my best friends and I went to the park because the weather was very beautiful. We saw a small dog that had no home, so we gave it some of our water and bread. I thinked the dog was hungry, so we bought some food in the store.

Later, we sat on the grass and ate some delicious sandwiches. We drank fresh juice and felt very happy together. My friend told a funny story and we laughed a lot. We spoke about our school and then we sang some favorite songs.

Suddenly, the dog runned after a ball. It was a great day! Finally, we came back home at 6:00 pm because we were very tired. I sleeped very well last night after the adventure.

An unforgetable data.(Emilio)
